## 1. Positioning

**Product name:** Builder Gold — Goalie Development Workbook
**One-liner:** An 8-module, coach-sign-off goalie development system that takes goalies from a repeatable stance to game-reading confidence — with proof of progress on every page.

**Who it's for:**
- Development-level and intermediate goalies ([INSERT AGE RANGE, e.g., 10–16])
- Goalie parents who want a structured plan instead of scattered drills
- Team and association coaches who need a turnkey goalie curriculum

**What makes it different (lead with these in all marketing):**
1. **Sign-off system** — every module ends in a measurable self-check and a coach signature. Parents and coaches can *see* progress, not just hope for it.
2. **Two-block progression** — Foundations before Reading the Game. No random drill lists; each module builds on the last.
3. **Built to be written in** — goal-setting, notes, and start/end self-ratings make it a season-long record, which also makes it hard to pirate and easy to re-buy for the next goalie or season.

**Brand fit:** Builder Gold sits inside the Builder series. Naming leaves room for a ladder: [Builder Bronze / Silver] below it and [Builder Platinum / Pro] above it.
